Who We Are

Young Resource Brigade (YRB) Foundation ,Government of India registered non-profit organisation is a group of dedicated Young Resources(YR's). We are providing a platform for students to make optimum development of their skills. We believes that Youth Education is Foundation for any Nation. We are dedicated for optimum utilisation of Youth Power in the Nation Development.

Our Mission

Our motive is to create Nation Builders.
To promote welfare activities related to Education And Research. As well To promote socially & economically backward students in various streams of Education. And to Utilized Youth Power for Nation development by boosting skill development.

Why We Focus on Skill Development

Nearly 80% of the Indian Workforce are unskilled for their job.
Introduction to skill training at a young age will by all means give the student an opportunity to explore various options and accordingly narrow down in his/her liking. There are also set of students who are not able to cope with main stream education because of economic reasons. Introducing skill traning at a young age will go a long way in directing those students to opportunities that will have a larger impact on their future. Introducing students to some employable skills will also help them to prepare and adapt for real work situation without much effort. It will ease the transition phase of being a student to a professional.
