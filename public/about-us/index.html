about

Young Resource Brigade (YRB) Foundation is a non-profit organisation established in 2019 with a vision of making students better through skill development. Providing them right platform and opportunities for optimum development in their skills. Educating students about digital technologies, building Enterprenuarship skills, providing students industry experiences and helping them to build their own start'ups are the primary goal of our organization.

Our Mission

Our motive is to create Nation Builders.
To promote welfare activities related to Education And Research. As well To promote socially & economically backward students in various streams of Education. And to Utilized Youth Power for Nation development by boosting skill development.
